# Product

## Register

product

## Users

People using an embedded wallet to understand their spendable balance, top up stablecoins, discover giftcards, and complete a mint or payment from a phone. They may understand crypto, but the interface should not require chain-level expertise for routine actions.

## Product Purpose

Mogate turns connected wallet balances into a direct giftcard checkout flow. The mobile product should make available funds, target network, payment readiness, and the next action immediately clear while preserving advanced routing and mint controls for users who need them.

## Brand Personality

Precise, confident, and energetic. HeroUI Native is the interaction reference: familiar controls, restrained surfaces, strong state communication, and a product-first hierarchy. Mogate's identity appears through its real mark and a warm orange accent, not decorative effects.

## Anti-references

Do not reproduce the current card-dashboard screenshot: oversized bordered containers, cards inside cards, placeholder text glyphs used as icons, cyan decoration, pastel gradients, floating controls, repeated uppercase eyebrows, or hero-sized metrics competing for attention.

## Design Principles

1. Put the current task before decoration.
2. Show money and transaction state with exact, scannable hierarchy.
3. Use familiar controls and one consistent component vocabulary.
4. Reveal chain and routing detail progressively without hiding it.
5. Earn attention with state and action, not ornamental color.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG 2.2 AA contrast, preserve Dynamic Type up to a practical mobile limit, provide visible and programmatic selected/disabled/loading states, maintain at least 44-by-44-point touch targets, never rely on color alone, and respect reduced-motion settings through HeroUI's motion controls.
